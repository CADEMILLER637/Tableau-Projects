Bombas E-Commerce Analytics Dashboard

## Overview
Developed a Tableau dashboard analyzing customer acquisition, conversion behavior, and first-order revenue across 1,000+ e-commerce transactions. The project maps the full customer funnel and identifies key opportunities to improve conversion and marketing performance.

## Business Problem
E-commerce brands need to understand where customers drop off in the purchasing process and when/where conversions are most likely to occur.

## Key Insights
- Identified major **conversion drop-off between add-to-cart and checkout**, highlighting a key opportunity for cart abandonment optimization  
- Found highest conversion rates during **midweek evenings (Tues–Thurs)**, supporting more effective campaign timing  
- Discovered **geographic regions with disproportionate revenue contribution**, enabling targeted marketing focus  
- Revealed **seasonal trends in first-order behavior**, informing acquisition strategy and budget allocation  

## Dashboard Features
- First-order revenue trends over time  
- Geographic distribution of customers  
- Monthly conversion patterns and cohort behavior  

## Business Impact
- Improves marketing ROI through better targeting and timing  
- Supports data-driven decisions for customer acquisition strategy  
- Identifies high-impact areas to increase conversion rates  

## Tools Used
- Tableau (Dashboard Design & Visualization)  
- Excel / Power Query (Data Preparation)
